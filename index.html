About Us
Our team consists of three hustlers with other technical team members. We are hustling for an idea that just came out of a random discussion .With hope in our eyes, working hard to make that idea into reality. More than making an idea into reality it's about giving a platform to people of India to maintain their health records safe at one digital platform. With advancement in technology we have seen that all the fields in this world have turned into Technology driven except one that is healthcare. In India, more than 70% of doctors use paper to write prescriptions and test centres use printed forms of providing the test reports. We are trying to create a platform where these doctors, these test centres and these hospitals could be empowered so that it benefits them as well as the patient.
